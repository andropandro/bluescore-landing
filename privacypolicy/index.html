Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us

Last updated: May 26, 2025

Overview

BlueScore is developed with respect for your privacy. We only collect data that is necessary for the app's functionality and store all information locally on your device.

Information Collected

  • Location searches you make
  • Weather data for selected locations
  • App settings and preferences
  • Technical log data for troubleshooting and support

Information Not Collected

  • Personally identifiable information
  • Account information or user data
  • Images, media files, or personal content
  • Continuous background location tracking
  • Personal messages or communications

Log Files and Troubleshooting

The app automatically creates log files on your device to assist with troubleshooting and support. These log files contain:

  • Technical information about the app's functions
  • Error messages and performance data
  • Timestamps of activities
  • Device information (iOS version, app version)

A new log file is created each time the app starts, and a maximum of five previous log files are automatically saved. The log files contain NO personal information such as names, places visited, or personal settings. Sensitive information such as API keys is automatically filtered out.

Sharing Log Files

You can voluntarily choose to share log files with our support team via the app's settings. This only happens when you actively choose to send an email with the log files attached. No log files are sent automatically or without your consent.

Data Management

All information is stored locally on your device. No data is sent to our servers or shared with third parties, except when you voluntarily choose to share log files for support.

Third-Party Services

We use the following external services to fetch weather and location information:

  • MET Norway API (weather data)
  • GeoNames API (location information)
  • OpenStreetMap/Overpass API (geographic data)
  • WeatherAPI.com (additional weather data)
  • Apple Maps & CoreLocation (location information)
  • Other public data services

These requests are made without sharing your personal information. Only location names and coordinates are sent to retrieve public weather and geographic information.

Analytics and Statistics

The app does NOT collect usage statistics, analytics, or telemetry. All information remains on your device.

Cache and Temporary Data

To improve performance, the app temporarily stores weather data and location information locally. This cache:

  • Is stored only on your device
  • Is automatically cleared after a certain time
  • Can be manually cleared via the app's settings
  • Contains no personal information

Your Rights

  • You can delete all stored information by uninstalling the app
  • You can clear cache and log files via the app's settings
  • You can choose not to share log files for support
  • You control what information is stored locally
